Space Wars 2D

About The Game

Blast through endless waves of aliens in Space Wars 2d! Upgrade your ship, unleash powerful abilities, and defeat bosses in this classic arcade space shooter. Take control of your spaceship and survive as long as you can!

Collect coins from defeated enemies and grab powerful boosts like shields and faster firing to help you last longer. Use your collected coins in the shop to unlock new, more powerful spaceships and upgrade your power-ups for your next flight. Set a new high score and become the best pilot in the galaxy!

How to Play

Shooting is always automatic, so all you have to worry about is expert piloting! Your job is to move around the screen to dodge enemy attacks and destroy waves of aliens.

  • For Desktop Users: Use your Mouse to control the ship. Move the cursor around the screen and your ship will follow.
  • For Mobile / Touchscreen: Use your Finger to control the ship. Touch the screen and drag to move.
